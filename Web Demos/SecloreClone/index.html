Data-Centric Security

The Seclore Difference

Your data is in constant motion. Once it leaves your enterprise — whether shared with a trusted third-party or leaked — it’s out of your control. We call this the data security gap, and we’re on a mission to fix it.
The Seclore platform takes a data-centric approach to security by giving you the ability to see, protect, and control your digital assets — wherever they go.

Know Your Risk

Seclore provides visibility into your enterprise’s most sensitive assets, who has access, what access they have, and what unauthorized actions have been blocked. Gain risk insights from interactive dashboards to granularly investigate data usage.
